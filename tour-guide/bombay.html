Historical Landmarks

  • Gateway of India

    An iconic arch-monument, the Gateway of India is a must-visit landmark in Mumbai.

  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this railway station is an excellent example of Victorian Gothic Revival architecture.

Cultural Attractions

  • Elephanta Caves

    These ancient rock-cut caves are located on Elephanta Island and are a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • National Gallery of Modern Art

    A leading art gallery that showcases modern and contemporary Indian art.

Entertainment Options

  • Marine Drive

    A scenic promenade along the coast, Marine Drive offers stunning views and is a popular spot for evening strolls.

  • Juhu Beach

    One of the most famous beaches in Mumbai, Juhu Beach is a great place to relax and enjoy street food.
