Beaches

  • Baga Beach

    One of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its vibrant nightlife and water sports.

  • Anjuna Beach

    Famous for its trance parties and flea market, Anjuna Beach is a must-visit for beach lovers.

Historical Landmarks

  • Bom Jesus Basilica

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this basilica is famous for housing the remains of St. Francis Xavier.
