Places to Visit in Amritsar
  • Historical Landmarks
  • Spiritual Destinations
  • Cultural Attractions

Historical Landmarks

  • Jallianwala Bagh

    A public garden that serves as a memorial to the victims of the 1919 massacre during British rule.

  • Partition Museum

    A museum dedicated to the history of the Partition of India in 1947.

Spiritual Destinations

  • Golden Temple

    The holiest shrine in Sikhism, the Golden Temple is renowned for its stunning architecture and serene atmosphere.

  • Durgiana Temple

    A prominent Hindu temple, also known as Lakshmi Narayan Temple, located in the city of Amritsar.
