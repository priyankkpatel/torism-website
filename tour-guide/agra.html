Historical Landmarks

  • Taj Mahal

    The iconic symbol of love, the Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

  • Agra Fort

    A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Agra Fort is a massive red sandstone fort that served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors.

Cultural Attractions

  • Fatehpur Sikri

    A historic city and UNESCO World Heritage Site, Fatehpur Sikri was built by Emperor Akbar and served as the Mughal capital.

  • Mehtab Bagh

    A beautiful garden complex located across the Yamuna River, offering stunning views of the Taj Mahal.
