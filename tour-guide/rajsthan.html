Places to Visit in Rajasthan

Historical Landmarks

  • Amber Fort

    A majestic fort overlooking Maota Lake, known for its artistic elements and architecture.

  • City Palace, Jaipur

    A royal residence with a blend of Mughal and Rajput architecture, located in the heart of Jaipur.

Cultural Attractions

  • Jantar Mantar

    A collection of astronomical instruments, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is an architectural marvel.

  • Hawa Mahal

    Known as the "Palace of Winds," Hawa Mahal is a five-story structure with over 900 windows.
